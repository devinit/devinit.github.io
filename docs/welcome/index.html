Welcome to the new home of Global Humanitarian Assistance programme

Jump to section

You've been redirected to our new website, which features reports and research from the Global Humanitarian Assistance (GHA) programme. Development Initiatives’ GHA programme analyses resource flows and programming targeting people at risk of and affected by crises, and promotes data transparency.

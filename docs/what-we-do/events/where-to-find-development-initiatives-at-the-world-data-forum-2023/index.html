Where to find Development Initiatives at the World Data Forum 2023

  • 24 April - 27 April 2023
  • Hangzhou, China, and online

The United Nations World Data Forum meets from 24 – 27 April in Hangzhou, China to stimulate data innovation, nurture partnerships, mobilise high-level political and financial support for data, and build a pathway to better data for sustainable development.

DI will be there in person, represented by Claudia Wells , International Hub Director, and Bernard Sabiti , Senior Strategic Partnerships & Engagement Manager. You can follow Bernard and Claudia on twitter.

Below is a list of events that DI is co-hosting and speaking at during the UN World Data Forum 2023 .

We hope you can join us, either in-person or virtually!

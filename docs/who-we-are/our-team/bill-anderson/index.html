Where I am based: London, UK

What I do: Content lead on our cross-cutting work on data landscaping and strengthening national data ecosystems, supported by our team of data scientists and information systems analysts. Oversight of the development of systems underpinning DI’s collection, storage and analysis of data and production of information.

Previous experience: 13 years as an intelligence analyst for the African National Congress of South Africa. 10 years as MD of a software house doing Electronic Data Interchange. Consultant specialising in public information systems in Africa.
